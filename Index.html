80s Theme

In 2017 and 2018 the 80s style theme became VERY popular. I remember being a pretty big fan of the VICE Miami Colour scheme and style. One of my favourite Games that represent this is Grand Theft Auto: Vice City. I have used that colour scheme in a lot of my work. Incase you aren't aware of the colours used they are Pink and Light blue or as I know them # E81DC9(Pink) and #154DCD(Blue).

Remembering that is my version/take on it so the colours are a little more saturated compared to the original. I have created a lot in this area, such as a few Vice City art and a recreation of the Vice City intro. The main reason Vice city is such a big thing for me is because it was the first game I ever played. And easily became my favourite game of all time!
